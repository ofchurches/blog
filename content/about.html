The present

I am a statistician working for the Government of South Australia. I started this job in 2017. I provide analyses of administrative data for internal government communications and public facing data visualisations.

I also provide advice to government on the use of quantitative information including the construction of automated decision making systems. My work in this field led me to a Churchill Fellowship in 2019 to look at ethical and just regulation of artificial intelligences.

I still collaborate with the CAIN Laboratory at UniSA. The beautiful knitted neurons on the landing page of this blog were made by the staff and students there.
